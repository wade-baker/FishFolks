Frequently Asked Questions

Here we answer some frequently asked questions.

Q: Can I use tap water in my fish tank?
A: Maybe, but first test for chlorine and ammonia.
Q: How do I know if the water temperature is correct?
A: Buy an inexpensive thermometer to test the water temperature.
Q: Is there a limit on how many fish I can have in a tank?
A: Yes. The general rule is to have 1 inch of fish per gallon of water to avoid sickness.
Q: Why does my water look green?
A: Algae is a natural part of a tank but if it gets out of hand it can make the water look green. If it gets out of hand, try buying an algae eater or a plant.
Q: Do I need to remove the fish from the tank for cleaning?
A: No. It is ideal for your fish to be moved as little as possible to ensure a happy fish friend.
